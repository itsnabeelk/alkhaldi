About Company

Established at 1972, Al-Khaldi Group by its founder Sheikh Mubarak Zaid Awad Al-Khaldi, the great entrepreneur and a man of visions. Eventually transformed from a sole proprietorship to one of the leading holding companies in Saudi Arabia by focusing on a diverse portfolio of around 25 companies spanning six of high-growth sectors in Saudi Arabia (Supply chain and logistics services, Oil and Gas, Drilling and Fuel trading, building material trading and contracting, Real estate services, Travel and tourism services and Retail). That is through adopting and enhancing a flexible, long-term, and sustainable investment approach to enhance the Kingdom’s Vision 2030 Today, we enjoy the patronage of several government, semi government entities and millions of consumers globally across consumer goods and services, real estate, technology, supply chain and logistics, fuel retail and different JV’s and many other businesses. In fact, our geographical footprint extends beyond the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. At the heart of all of this is our people. We take much pride in fostering, inspiring and paying attention workplace, with an agile and high-performance culture. We are also deeply committed to recognizing and valuing diversity across our teams.
