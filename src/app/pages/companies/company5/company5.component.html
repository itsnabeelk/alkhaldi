Auto Gulf Company

Founded in 2012 by Al-Khaldi Holding Company, AGC has grown into a thriving world of excellence and accomplishments, where customer satisfaction, innovation, convenience, and vast product choice are the order of the day. Based in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia, AGC has had an immense success in carving its name in the market since 2012, dealing with major clients and suppliers of vehicles in the Gulf region and beyond, and continues to be a leading pioneer in the full spectrum of the automotive business. AGC’s primary goal is to provide clients with a one-stop convenience-based platform for automotive services supporting various industries in respect of our clients’ needs. ACG’s substantial range of consultation and commercial services, divided amongst numerous brands, offers clients a wide variety of solutions for their corporate and commercial needs. Our product lines offer an unmatched selection of vehicular products and auto services, ranging from industry grade specialized equipment to latest, most luxurious premium vehicles. At ACG, we strongly believe that partnering with an internationally renowned industry leaders will definitely take successful business to even greater heights, Thus, we are proud of our partnership with the most reliable automotive brands in the world.
