Who We Are

Established at 1972, Al-Khaldi Group by its founder Sheikh Mubarak Zaid Awad Al-Khaldi, the great entrepreneur and a man of visions. Eventually transformed from a sole proprietorship to one of the leading holding companies in Saudi Arabia by focusing on a diverse portfolio of around 25 companies spanning six of high-growth sectors in Saudi Arabia (Supply chain and logistics services, Oil and Gas, Drilling and Fuel trading, building material trading and contracting, Real estate services, Travel and tourism services and Retail). That is through adopting and enhancing a flexible, long-term, and sustainable investment approach to enhance the Kingdom’s Vision 2030
